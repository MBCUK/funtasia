Funtasia Festival

On: 28th July 2013
11am - 7pm

At: Los Altos park, Sandown.

Funtasia is a Family Friendly Day Festival, Featuring:

  • A Woodland Stage with lots of Island Bands.

  • A fun filled entertainment arena

  • Separate children's tent with a variety of children's entertainment and workshops.

  • Many stall holders, Side shows, food vendors, licenced bar and lots more!!
